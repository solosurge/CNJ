CNJ Bible Study was created with the goal of deepening believers understanding of the scripture. In depth knowledge of the scripture is a fundamental and essential part of the spiritual maturity and growth of a Christian. The Bible tells all believers to "go on and get past the elementary stage in the teachings and doctrine of Christ, advancing steadily toward the completeness and perfection that belong to spiritual maturity." Hebrews 6:1 As we advance in our knowledge of Christ "we may no longer be little children, being blown and carried about by every wind of doctrine...but speaking the truth in love, we may grow up into Him in all things." Ephesians 4:14-15 It is our desire to be faithful stewards of the manifold grace of God presenting "every man fullgrown in Christ Jesus." Colossians 1:28
